Automatically triage incoming customer support tickets from our Zendesk queue.
Classify each ticket by urgency (P1/P2/P3), category (billing/technical/general),
and estimated resolution time.
P1 tickets should immediately page the on-call engineer via PagerDuty.
P2 tickets should be assigned to the right team queue automatically.
P3 tickets should get an auto-response with relevant FAQ links based on the ticket content.
Log all triage decisions with confidence scores to a PostgreSQL database
for weekly quality review.
